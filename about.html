About Nompumelelo Mathabela

Frontend Developer based in Johannesburg, South Africa

Nompumelelo P. Mathabela is an African Black Queer Feminist activist, digital activist, front-end developer, women human rights defender, feminist artivist, tech lover, an entrepreneur. I believe that technology can change the world for the better, it can contribute to ending gender-based violence, and that every young girl should learn how to code. I am passionate about coding and I want to be able to use my skills in building apps and software that can assist in the fight for social justice in my country and globally.
